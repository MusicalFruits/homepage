Our venue is a heritage building that has not been adapted fully for all bodies. There is a small step up to a courtyard space that has a ramp that can enter the dance floor.
It is level access throughout however there is no accessible toilet facilities. If you have any questions please email us. musicalfruitsparty {A/T} gmail {d0T} com
